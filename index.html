RAFTING

The first step in the wonderful waves of the Dora Baltea river. A trip that combines beautiful and natural scenery plus action, to be enjoyed by anyone. This is the choice for everybody who wants to enjoy a nonstop paddling and all the fun in a natural environment.

KAYAKING

The warm, clear water that turns teal blue when light hits the deeper pools is bordered by tall palm trees and vines. If you want to explore by kayak, visit outside the winter manatee season, when the waters are filled with these gentle creatures and boating is restricted to protect them..

HOTEL ROOMS

The accommodations at the Hotel Room have a contemporary design and have central heating. Rooms have a 26-inch plasma satellite TV, a desk and a private bathroom with a hairdryer. Breakfasts are served every morning and the hotel restaurant offers a modern and varied menu from Monday to Saturday. Furthermore, there are several bars, restaurants and shops within a 5-minute walk..
